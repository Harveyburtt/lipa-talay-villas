Lipa Noi

The Quiet Side of Samui

Located on the unspoilt western coast of Koh Samui, Lipa Noi is renowned for its calm, clear waters and spectacular sunsets over the Gulf of Thailand. Away from the bustle of Chaweng, this is Samui as it used to be.

The estate is a short drive from Nathon town for local markets and amenities, and approximately 30 minutes from Samui International Airport.
